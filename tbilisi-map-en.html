🦠 Prometheus Caves
Kutaisi / Tskaltubo — ~240 km, ~3 h by car or train to Kutaisi. Georgia's largest cave system: 1420 m route, 6 illuminated halls with classical music. Optional boat ride on an underground river. Ticket ~€2.7. Plan 1–2 days.
🦕 Sataplia Nature Reserve
Near Prometheus Caves, Kutaisi. Dinosaur footprints. Combine with the caves — one day trip from Kutaisi.
⛪ Jvari Monastery
Mtskheta — ~20 km, ~30 min by car. 6th century. Taxi only: €9–16. Best combined with Mtskheta (Svetitskhoveli Cathedral).
